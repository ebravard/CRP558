I explored multiple features when making my new customized Mapbox Tile Layer. First, in the map you see above, I selected the Basic base map with the Seashore variation. Next, I increased font size of Points of Interest to 25% and changed the font type of Points of Interest to EB Garamond Bold. I also changed the color of greenspaces to a slightly darker green color and changed the Road Network component from Simple to Navigation.

For my new layers, I added “road” from Mapbox Streets v8 Source by filtering the feature type to equal “bike_road” to show all roads that have a bike lane on them. I made this bike_road layer a dark red color and made the width of the line 1.25px. The other layer I obtained from Mapbox Streets v8 Source was filtered by “class” to equal “path” to designate sidewalks on campus. I changed the sidewalks to a turquoise color. I also chose to incorporate an outside layer that I made using geojson.io. The geoJSON layer created shows major bike racks located on Iowa State University's campus. I uploaded this bike rack layer, changed the color to dark purple, made line width 1.3px, and put the bike rack layer on top of all the other layers.
